SHMOOD

Shmood allows users to create an account and upload images to their own personal mood board. They can login and out as they please, and clear their boards if they need a clean slate. Also, the homepage draws from user profiles to create a randomized board.

Made with:

Node.js, React, Redis, mongoDB, Handlebars, Bootstrap, Express, HTML5, CSS, and a partner

CoinCounter

CoinCounter is an app made to help students with special needs visualize money. It has both a money visualizer and change calculator, and shows numerical dollar and change amounts in simple graphics of US currency. Also, there are 3D turnarounds of each currency piece, as well as some fun educational facts.

Made with:

Bootstrap, jQuery, Photoshop, three.js, HTML5, CSS, and a team

Best Multimedia Hack

bookWatch

Users can enter the title of a book and recieve a list of curated movie recommendations. The recommendations list can have up to 15 movies, and provides a short description, the rating, and how the movie scored in reviews.

Made with:

Vue.js, Bootstrap, HTML5, CSS, Firebase, OpenLibrary API, TasteDive API, OMDb API, and a partner

DUSK

An Interative graphics site created using the three.js javascript library. DUSK uses skysperes, water geometry, lighting, and audio to create three interactive sunset scenes. Users can view each scene with a 360 panoramic camera, and directional audio is used depending on distance from the water.

Made with:

jQuery, Photoshop, three.js, HTML5, and CSS

plantBar

plantBar allows users to enter in their plants and when they were last watered. This creates a collection that reminds them when to water each plant.
