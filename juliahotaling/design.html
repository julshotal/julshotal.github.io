575

575 is a magnetic poetry app where users can choose from 4 nature-themed word sets and drag/delete individual words to write poems. The background image of the app can be uploaded by the user, and the share button allows users to share their creations. In addition, RiTa.js is used for a syllable-checker popup.

Made with:

Swift, XCode, Photoshop, and RiTa.js
