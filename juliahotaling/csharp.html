Bug Destroyer

A remake of the beloved arcade game Asteroids, the player controls a lizard using the keyboard to destroy the bugs on screen. When hit, each bug breaks into three smaller ones. The size of the bug hit gains the player different points, but being hit will cost them one of their three lives.

Made with:

Unity 2D, C#, Photoshop

Humans vs Zombies: Underwater

This 3D simulation uses autonomous agents to mimic predator/prey behavior. Different forces of seeking vs fleeing act on the sharks and minnows, as well as the desire to avoid obstacles. When a minnow is caught by a shark, it becomes another shark.

Made with:

Unity 3D, C#, Photoshop
